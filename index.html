Mission Statement

   The APP Foundation is a clandestine organization committed to locating, securing, and studying anomalies that challenge the natural order. Our mission is to safeguard humanity while uncovering the secrets of these extraordinary phenomena.

  • - Analyze: Investigate and document all known anomalies.
  • - Protect: Ensure containment to prevent harm.
  • - Preserve: Maintain the delicate balance between the normal and the anomalous.
