Community Contributions


The APP Foundation project has sparked the creativity of people all around the globe, and has harboured a community of talented individuals that have made incredible contributions, from fan fiction, to fan art, and even fan-made APPs.
This page serves as a highlight of the most recent of those contributions. We sincerely thank all who made The APP Foundation what it is today.
